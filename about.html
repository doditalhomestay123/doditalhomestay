Welcome

Our Story

Discover the serene beauty of Agora, Dodital. Our family-run homestay offers a warm and authentic experience in the heart of nature. For the past 5 years, we’ve been sharing our rich Ghardawali culture, ancient wisdom, and captivating village tales with our guests. Experience the tranquility of our cozy accommodations and savor delicious, home-cooked organic meals. Immerse yourself in the stunning landscapes, embark on thrilling treks, and connect with the local community. Join us and create unforgettable memories in the pristine beauty of Dodital.

A Bit of History

Deep-Rooted in the Community Our family has been an integral part of the Agora Village community for generations. We’re proud to share our rich Ghardawali heritage and traditions with our guests. Our roots run deep, and we're committed to preserving our cultural legacy. We embody the spirit of community, where we work together to build homes, celebrate weddings, and care for the weak family and people. By promoting heritage tourism, utilizing technology to document our traditions, and sharing stories, we aim to keep our culture alive for future generations.

Your Commitment to Hospitality

A Warm and Caring Host We believe in providing exceptional hospitality, treating our guests like family. Our 24-hour guest service ensures your comfort and well-being. From cozy, clean rooms to friendly service, we strive to create a memorable stay. Eco-Conscious and Community-Minded We’re deeply committed to preserving our environment and supporting our community. We’ve adopted eco-friendly practices passed down through generations, including sustainable living and waste reduction. We understand the importance of every tree and work tirelessly to maintain our lush forests and farm. We invite our guests to connect with our small community of around 500 people. By interacting with locals, you can gain valuable insights into our culture and traditions.

Spacious Accommodations for a Comfortable Stay

Our homestay can comfortably accommodate 10-12 people.
Upper Level:
Balcony: Enjoy breathtaking views of the village, waterfall, and forest. Library: Relax with a good book. Bedrooms: Two semi-double rooms and one large room with two semi-king size beds, one single bed, and a sofa. All rooms have attached western-style bathrooms.
Lower Level:
Bedrooms: Three semi-double rooms and one room with bunk beds for four people. All rooms have attached western-style bathrooms. Note: The lower level rooms are primarily for our family's use. Comfortable Accommodations Our homestay offers a variety of comfortable rooms, each equipped with an attached bathroom and essential amenities like beds, pillows, towels, and toiletries.

Unforgettable Local Experiences

Immerse yourself in the local culture and enjoy unique experiences: Fire-Cooked Feasts: Savor delicious meals cooked over an open fire, using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Community Interactions: Interact with friendly locals and learn about their traditions and way of life. Guided Treks: Explore the stunning natural beauty of the region with our knowledgeable guides. Cultural Workshops: Participate in cultural activities like traditional dance, music, and crafts. Tea Time Traditions: Enjoy a warm cup of tea offered by the local families.
