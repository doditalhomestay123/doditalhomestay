Mountain-Style Food

Enjoy delicious home-cooked meals prepared in a traditional mountain kitchen with a chulha (wood stove). Experience the warmth of local flavors and organic ingredients.

Mountain View Rooms

Wake up to breathtaking views of the Himalayas. Our cozy rooms offer a panoramic look at the majestic mountains and waterfalls right from your room.

Bonfire Evenings

Gather around a warm bonfire under the starry sky. Perfect for storytelling, music, and soaking in the tranquil mountain vibes.

Hot Water Service

Enjoy hot water service for a comfortable bath even in the cold mountain weather

Pocket WiFi Connectivity
